Exploring the Solar System

The Solar System consists of the Sun, eight planets, moons, asteroids, and comets. Each planet is unique in its size, composition, and distance from the Sun.

For example, Earth is the only planet known to harbor life, while Jupiter is the largest planet in our system.

Venturing into Deep Space
