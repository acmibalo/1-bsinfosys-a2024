Tower of God


Summary

Twenty-Fifth Bam, has spent most of his life in the dark, confined space of a cave. His only companion was a girl named Rachel, who one day decides to climb the Tower in order to see the stars. After Rachel disappears into the Tower, Bam follows her, desperate to find her and reunite. However, the Tower is not just a physical structure—it's a dangerous and complex place, with each floor offering its own challenges, traps, and powerful individuals.

The Tower is divided into many floors, and each floor is like a self-contained world with its own set of rules and rulers. To ascend, climbers must pass a series of tests to prove their worth. On his journey, Bam meets various characters, including Khun Aguero Agnis, a skilled and strategic individual, and Rak Wraithraiser, a strong but eccentric creature. They form alliances and face a wide variety of enemies, each with their own motivations.

As Bam climbs the Tower, he begins to uncover secrets about its true purpose, the mysterious forces controlling it, and his own origin. The deeper he goes, the more dangerous and complex the Tower becomes, as he discovers that many of the climbers have their own dark agendas. Throughout his journey, Bam must confront not only external challenges but also his own identity and the nature of his connection to the Tower.
