Bubbles


Summary

Hibiki, a young parkour athlete, who is part of a group of individuals who survive in the gravity-defying Tokyo, engaging in dangerous stunts and parkour competitions. He is saved from death by a mysterious girl named Uta, who has the ability to control bubbles. Uta has a unique and mysterious connection to the strange phenomenon that caused the city’s collapse. As Hibiki and Uta work together, they uncover secrets about the bubbles and the events that led to Tokyo’s downfall, leading them to confront deeper themes about the connection between humans and the world around them.
