The Great Cleric


Summary

Right as he was about to earn a promotion, a salaryman meets meets an untimely demise. Taking pity, a mysterious being grants him a second life in the land of Galdiardia as a teengager. Choosing the name Luciel for himself and a role as a healer, the young man sets off for the nearby town of Merratoni. There, he learns that healers are a coveted resource, yet they often take advantage of others for monetary gain. Vowing to be different from other healers, he joins the local branch of the Healer's Guild and begins unlocking magical potential.

After failing to save someone with his magic, Luciel is determined to get stronger. To improve his physical strenght and hone his magic skills, he registers as an adventurer and takes rigorous training under Brod, the chief of the Adventurer's Guild. Soon, Luciel gradually finds his way through Galdardia, gaining a reputation as a selfless healer who iads others reagardless of expense.
