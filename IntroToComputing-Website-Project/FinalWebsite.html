About Me

Hi there! My Name is Trisha Nicole Silvestre, and I’m a first-year student at the University of Southern Mindanao, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Systems. As part of our final project for the first semester, I’ve created this convenience store-inspired website to provide you with a seamless and enjoyable one-stop-shop experience.

My inspiration for creating this website sparked from my background as a former Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) student. During my studies, I discovered my passion and interest for the business and marketing field. To showcase my skills and bring my ideas to life, I dedicated time to designing the products and creating this platform.

At Food Craze Express, I am committed to offering a wide range of high-quality products, making your shopping experience both convenient and efficient.

Thank you for visiting and I hoped you enjoy exploring Food Craze Express!

About this Website

Welcome to Food Craze Express!

At Food Craze Express, we are dedicated to providing you an online shopping experience that is easy and convenient at the same time. Our website is designed to be a convenient one-stop-shop, offering a wide range of food products from beverages to snacks and breakfast items.

Here’s a glimpse of what you’ll find inside our website:

Gourmet Coffees: Experience the art of coffee with our premium blends. Discover single-origin coffees, flavored blends, and everything you need to brew the perfect cup.

Breakfast (Yogurts & Cereals): Start your day right with our delicious breakfast options. Explore everything from flavorful and nutritious cereals to the creamy taste of yogurts.

Desserts (Sweet Treats & Frozen Desserts): Indulge in our delightful selection of sweet treats. Whether you're craving some chocolate, cake, or ice cream, you'll find a variety of desserts to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Snacks (Nuts & Chips): Satisfy your cravings with our tasty snacks. From chips and mixed nuts to crunchy snacks that makes every bite enjoyable, we offer a variety of junk food to enjoy anytime, anywhere.

Beverages (Soft drinks & Juices): Quench your thirst with our wide range of refreshing drinks. From soft drinks and juices to energy drinks and bottled water, we've got all your beverage needs covered.

Explore our different categories, take advantage of our deals, and stay updated with the latest product offerings and tips.
