KIER BENEDICT BAHAYNON

I’m Kier Benedict, a multimedia creative with skills in graphic design, photography, videography, art, and journalism. I’m currently a member of The Mindanao Tech, and I’ve previously served as the head of Graphic Design and as a sub-photographer and videographer for Spectrum Creatives at NDMU IBED. I’m also a proud member of Pitik South Cotabato and regularly contribute to Manila Bulletin, Daily Inquirer, and Mindanews. My work revolves around blending creativity and storytelling, allowing me to explore different mediums and connect with diverse audiences.
