EXPLORING CORON ISLAND

Activities to Enjoy on Coron Island, Palawan, Philippines

Coron Island offers a wide range of activities that highlight its natural beauty and rich marine biodiversity. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural immersion, there’s something for everyone. Here’s a list of must-try activities on Coron Island:

1. Explore Kayangan Lake

Dubbed the "cleanest lake in Asia," Kayangan Lake is a serene spot surrounded by towering limestone cliffs. Visitors can: Swim or snorkel in its crystal-clear waters. Hike up to the famous viewpoint for panoramic views of the lagoon and cliffs.

2. Dive into Barracuda Lake

A favorite among divers, Barracuda Lake is known for its thermocline layers, where warm and cold water meet. Activities include: Scuba diving to explore its unique underwater rock formations. Snorkeling to experience the lake's surreal tranquility.

3. Shipwreck Diving

Coron Bay is a world-renowned dive destination with multiple WWII Japanese shipwrecks. Activities include: Scuba diving to explore the wrecks, now vibrant ecosystems teeming with marine life. Snorkeling above shallower wrecks like the Lusong Gunboat.

4. Island Hopping

Coron is surrounded by picturesque islands with white-sand beaches and turquoise waters. Popular spots include: Malcapuya Island for sunbathing and swimming. Banana Island and Bulog Dos Island for snorkeling and picnics.

5. Snorkeling and Coral Exploration

Discover the vibrant coral reefs and marine life around the island: Visit Twin Lagoon, where saltwater and freshwater meet. Snorkel in Siete Pecados Marine Park, a protected area brimming with colorful fish and corals.

6. Relax on Pristine Beaches

Coron Island features secluded beaches perfect for relaxation: Enjoy a quiet retreat at Smith Beach or CYC Beach. Take a beach picnic while soaking in the stunning views.

7. Kayaking and Paddleboarding

Paddle around tranquil lagoons or explore hidden coves by renting a kayak or paddleboard.

From its clear lakes and coral reefs to its rich cultural heritage, Coron Island offers endless activities that cater to a variety of interests. Whether you're exploring underwater treasures or simply basking in the island’s natural beauty, Coron is an unforgettable destination.
