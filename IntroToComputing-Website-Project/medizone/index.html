Over-the-Counter Drugs

Over-the-counter medications (OTC) have become vital part of our healthcare and drug lanscape.

  • Sudafed® (pseudoephedrine)
  • Aleve® (naproxen sodium)
  • Benadryl® (diphenhydramine)
  • Melatonin
  • Pepto-Bismol® (bismuth subsalicylate)

Sudafed® (pseudoephedrine)

SUDAFED® Sinus Congestion Maximum Strength Decongestant Tablets provide powerful, non-drowsy relief from sinus pressure and nasal congestion. Each tablet contains 30 mg of pseudoephedrine HCl, a nasal decongestant that helps reduce nasal swelling and inflammation to improve ease of breathing.

Dosage:
Sinus Congestion (30 mg/ tablet)

Amount per dosing:
Take 2 caplets every 4 to 6 hours.

Do not Exceed:
Do not take more than 8 caplets in 24 hours.

Aleve® (naproxen sodium)

When you're tired of taking and retaking pills every few hours to deal with long-lasting aches and pains, Aleve can help. Just 2 caplets are strong enough to provide all day pain relief.

Dosage:
220 mg/ tablet, caplet, gelcap or liquid gel

Amount per dosing:
Take one tablet, caplet, gelcap or liquid gel every 8 to 12 hours while symptoms last. For the first dose, you may take 2 pills within the first hour.

Do not Exceed:
Do not exceed more than 2 tablets, caplets, gelcaps or liquid gels in 12 hours, and do not exceed 3 tablets, caplets, gelcaps or liquid gels in 24 hours. The smallest effective dose should be used. Drink a full glass of water with each dose.
Do not take for more than 10 consecutive days for pain or 3 days for fever, unless directed by a doctor..

Benadryl® (diphenhydramine)

Use BENADRYL® Allergy ULTRATABS® Tablets with 25 mg of diphenhydramine HCI, which are antihistamine allergy tablets, for effective relief from both indoor and outdoor allergies.

Dosage:
25 mg/tablet

Amount per dosing:
Take 1 to 2 tablets every 4 to 6 hours or as directed by a doctor.

Do not Exceed:
Do not take more than 6 doses in 24 hours. Use only as directed.

Melatonin

Melatonin is a hormone that helps maintain the body’s circadian rhythm, our internal clock that affects sleep, hormones, and other body functions.

Dosage:
1-10 mg/ tablet

Amount per dosing:
0.5 mg up to 5 mg

Do not Exceed:
Do not exceed nine months of taking for light difficulty falling asleep.

Pepto-Bismol® (bismuth subsalicylate)

This medicine is used for heartburn and acid reflux, indigestion, diarrhoea and feeling sick (nausea). It works by protecting your stomach and the lower part of your food pipe from stomach acid.

Dosage:
Original or Rapid Relief LiquiCaps (262 mg/ chewable tablet, caplet or LiquiCap)

Amount per dosing:
Take 2 tablets as needed.

Do not Exceed:
Maximum daily dose: 8 regular-strength (262 mg) doses or 4 extra-strength (525 mg) doses. Do not use for more than 2 days.

Nexlizet

Generic name: BEMPEDOIC ACID 180mg, EZETIMIBE 10mg

Dosage form: tablet, film coated

Drug class: Antihyperlipidemic combinations

Recommended Dosage and Administration

The recommended dosage of NEXLIZET is one tablet orally once daily. One tablet of NEXLIZET contains 180 mg of bempedoic acid and 10 mg of ezetimibe. Swallow the tablet whole. NEXLIZET can be taken with or without food. If a dose is missed, take the missed dose as soon as possible. Do not double the next dose.

After initiation of NEXLIZET, analyze lipid levels within 8 to 12 weeks

Gemtesa

Generic name: vibegron 75mg

Dosage form: tablet, film coated

Drug class: Urinary antispasmodics

Recommended Dosage

The recommended dosage of GEMTESA is one 75 mg tablet orally, once daily with or without food. Swallow GEMTESA tablets whole with a glass of water.

In adults, GEMTESA tablets also may be crushed, mixed with a tablespoon (approximately 15 mL) of applesauce and taken immediately with a glass of water.

Qulipta

Generic name: ATOGEPANT 10mg

Dosage form: tablet

Drug class: CGRP inhibitors

Recommended Dosage

QULIPTA is taken orally with or without food

Episodic Migraine

The recommended dosage of QULIPTA for episodic migraine is 10 mg, 30 mg, or 60 mg taken once daily.

Chronic Migraine

The recommended dosage of QULIPTA for chronic migraine is 60 mg taken once daily.

Tepezza

Generic name: TEPROTUMUMAB 500mg

Dosage form: injection, powder, lyophilized, for solution

Drug class: Growth hormone receptor blockers

Recommended Dosage

The recommended dose of TEPEZZA is an intravenous infusion of 10 mg/kg for the initial dose followed by an intravenous infusion of 20 mg/kg every three weeks for 7 additional infusions.

Time to sleep onset may be delayed if taken with or soon after a meal.

Quviviq

Generic name: daridorexant 25mg

Dosage form: tablet, film coated

Drug class: Miscellaneous anxiolytics, sedatives and hypnotics

Recommended Dosage

The recommended dosage range is 25 mg to 50 mg of QUVIVIQ taken orally no more than once per night within 30 minutes of going to bed (with at least 7 hours remaining prior to planned awakening).

Time to sleep onset may be delayed if taken with or soon after a meal.
