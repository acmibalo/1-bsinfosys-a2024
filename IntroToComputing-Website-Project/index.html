In memory of Technoblade

THE KING NEVER DIES

June 1999 - June 2022

Alexander (June 1, 1999 – June 2022), known online as Technoblade (/ˈtɛk.noʊˌbleɪd/ TEK-noh-blayd), was an American YouTuber known for his Minecraft videos, livestreams, and involvement in the Dream SMP. Technoblade registered his main channel on YouTube in 2013. His videos consisted primarily of Minecraft gameplay, particularly on the minigame server Hypixel. After rising to popularity in 2019 for his performances in player versus player (PvP) events, Technoblade was invited to the Dream SMP Minecraft server in 2020, further increasing his following.

LONG LIVE THE KING

Mr. Technodad, the father of the legendary Minecraft YouTuber Technoblade, has become a symbol of strength and resilience as he continues to carry on his son's legacy. Following the heartbreaking passing of Technoblade in 2022, Mr. Technodad has taken on the responsibility of keeping the memory of his son alive while honoring his incredible contributions to the gaming community.

GONE BUT NEVER FORGOTTEN

Technoblade’s passing was not just the end of a content creator, but the loss of a legend who had touched the hearts of millions. His legacy continues through the voices of those who loved him and the impact he left behind.

Wilbur Soot

Minecraft Youtuber

"I can’t even imagine a world without him in it. He was a force, both online and off. He made us laugh, he made us think, he made us all better people just by being who he was. I’ll never forget the moments we shared, and I will continue to carry that with me. Rest in peace, Techno."

Philza

Minecraft Youtuber

"I don’t even know what to say. I… I just wish I could’ve done more. I wish I could’ve been there more for him. But he… he was so strong. He fought so hard. He made us all proud, and I will miss him forever. We lost a legend. I lost a brother."

TommyInnit

Minecraft Youtuber

"He was more than just a friend. He was like a big brother to me. I… I don’t know what I’m supposed to do now. Technoblade didn’t deserve this. He was a warrior to the end, and he never gave up. I’ll miss him so much, but I know he’s proud of everything we’ve done."

Dream

Minecraft Youtuber

"Technoblade wasn’t just a friend, he was a brother. His strength, both in the game and in life, inspired me in ways I can’t fully describe. I’ll cherish our memories forever—his laughs, adventures, and the moments we shared. Rest in peace, Techno. Your legacy will continue to inspire us all."

Tubbo

Minecraft Youtuber

"Technoblade’s passing has left a huge gap in my heart. He wasn’t just a fellow creator, but family. His kindness, wisdom, and relentless spirit made him one of a kind. I’ll forever miss his presence, but his legacy and inspiration will live on. I’ll continue honoring him every day."

While not every message could be included here, it’s clear that everyone who knew Technoblade shared the same love, admiration, and grief. His impact reached far beyond just a few, and the entire community feels the void left by his passing. Whether through words, actions, or memories, Technoblade’s legacy continues to inspire and unite us all. We may not have captured every voice, but rest assured, everyone is mourning together, keeping his spirit alive in their hearts.
