About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I'm Rimdel Joe T. Anito, and I'm passionate on gaming, especialy MLBB. Since 2024, I've been dedicated to help MLBB players know more on how to play MLBB better and avoid toxic behaviors in-game. I believe that a healthy game will bring all players the happiness and joy they have been wanting in the MLBB field. Through my work, I strive to inspire other players to be more friendly with their team-mates and feel the game more rather than fighting with them. Whether you're here to judge my website or to serve this as a guide for your personal website, I'm thrilled to have you here. Thank you for visiting, and I hope you find something that resonates with you. Let's connect and create something amazing together!!
