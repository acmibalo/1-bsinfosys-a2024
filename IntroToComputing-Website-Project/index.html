HERO Pudge

Pudge is a Dota 2 hero known for his Meat Hook, which pulls enemies, Rot, dealing damage around him, and Dismember, a disabling attack. He’s tough, unpredictable, and a fan favorite in fights.

HERO Queen of Pain

Queen of Pain is a Dota 2 hero known for her high burst damage and mobility. She uses Shadow Strike to slow and damage enemies, Blink to teleport, Scream of Pain for area damage, and Sonic Wave to deal massive damage in a straight line. She's agile and great at harassing foes.

HERO Nature's Prophet

Nature's Prophet is a versatile Dota 2 hero who excels at pushing and global presence. He can teleport anywhere with Teleportation, summon tree minions with Nature's Call, and deal damage in a chain with Wrath of Nature. He's great at split-pushing and farming.
