SpyXFamily


Summary

A highly skilled secret spy who works for a government agency in a fictional, war-torn world. In order to complete his latest mission, which requires infiltrating a prestigious school and gaining access to a key political figure, he is tasked with forming a fake family. To maintain his cover, he adopts a young girl named Anya, who unbeknownst to him, is a telepath, and marries a woman named Yor, who is secretly an assassin.

As the story unfolds, the "Forger" family begins to live together, each member hiding their true identity from one another. Twilight (alias "Loid Forger") strives to keep his spy work a secret while navigating family life, Yor balances her dangerous job as an assassin with her role as a loving wife and mother, and Anya tries to help her "parents" with her telepathic powers, though she often misinterprets their intentions. Despite their hidden lives, the family gradually forms real emotional bonds, and each member grows to care for the others.
