London

London is the capital city of England. It is a vibrant metropolis with a rich history and diverse culture. It is also the most populous city in the United Kingdom, with a metropolitan area of over 13 million inhabitants. Besides english, more than 250 languages are spoken in London, reflecting its diverse population

Standing on the River Thames, London has been a major settlement for two millennia, its history going back to its founding by the Romans, who named it Londinium. London’s blend of historical depth, cultural richness, and modern innovation makes it a truly unique and dynamic city.

Did you know? Legend has it that if the ravens leave the Tower of London, the kingdom will fall. To prevent this, their wings are clipped.

Paris

Paris is the capital city of France. Paris is a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. The city is renowned for its cafes, bistros, and bakeries, offering everything from croissants to haute cuisine.

Paris is a city that captivates the hearts of millions of visitors each year with its timeless charm and endless opportunities for exploration. Whether you’re strolling along the Seine, enjoying a café au lait at a sidewalk café, or marveling at the art in the Louvre, Paris offers an unforgettable experience.

Did you know? The Notre Dame Cathedral is considered the point zero from which all distances in Paris are measured.

Tokyo

Tokyo is the bustling capital of Japan. Tokyo is a cultural hub, offering a mix of traditional and contemporary experiences. The city is known for its vibrant arts scene, including traditional Kabuki theater, modern art galleries, and anime culture.

Tokyo is a city of contrasts, where ancient temples stand alongside futuristic skyscrapers, and serene gardens provide a respite from the bustling urban life. Whether you’re exploring its historic sites, indulging in its culinary delights, or experiencing its cutting-edge technology, Tokyo offers something for everyone.

Did you know? The Henn Na Hotel in Tokyo is operated by robots, including a dinosaur robot at the reception.
