Technoblade: The King Never Dies

Alexander (June 1, 1999 – June 2022), known online as Technoblade, was an American YouTuber who became famous for his Minecraft videos and involvement in the Dream SMP.

Even in his last moments, Technoblade’s words remain unforgettable: "The King Never Dies."
