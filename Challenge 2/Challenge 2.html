Paddington Bear's London Adventure

Learn more about Paddington Bear

Arrival at Paddington Station

Paddington arrives at Paddington Station from Peru,
carrying a suitcase and wearing a tag that reads,
"Please look after this bear. Thank you."

Settling into the Brown Household

The Brown family finds Paddington, offers him help, and decides to take him home.
Paddington explores the house, meets Mrs. Bird, and experiences a mishap in the bathroom
that ends with him getting covered in soap and water.

Exploring London

Paddington explores London with the help of Mr. Gruber,
who becomes his friend and guide.
