Top 10 Must-Visit Spots in Cebu

1. Cebu Beach
Cebu is known for its stunning beaches, including those in Mactan, Malapascua, Bantayan, and Moalboal. Perfect for swimming, lounging, and water activities.

2. Cebu Sunset
Cebu offers breathtaking sunset spots, especially along its coastline. Some of the best views are found in areas like Mactan and Oslob.

3. Whale Shark Diving in Cebu
Oslob is famous for whale shark diving, where you can swim with these gentle giants in their natural habitat. It's a must-do activity when in Cebu.

4. Cebu Cultural Heritage
Explore the rich history of Cebu with its historic landmarks like Magellan’s Cross, Fort San Pedro, and the Basilica Minore del Santo Niño, reflecting its Spanish colonial past.

5. Mactan Island
Mactan Island is famous for its luxury resorts, beautiful beaches, and historical sites such as the Lapu-Lapu Monument and the Mactan Shrine.

6. Historical Jesuit Heritage in Cebu
Discover Cebu's Jesuit heritage through historic landmarks like Casa Gorordo Museum, showcasing the Spanish influence on Cebu’s culture and architecture.

7. Cebu Waterfalls
Cebu is home to beautiful waterfalls like Kawasan Falls, Tumalog Falls, and Dao Falls, perfect for adventure seekers and nature lovers.

8. Sirao Garden (Little Amsterdam)
Known as "Little Amsterdam," Sirao Garden offers colorful flowers and breathtaking views of Cebu. It's a popular photo spot for visitors.

9. Cebu Diving Sites
Cebu is famous for its world-class diving spots, including Moalboal, Malapascua, and Pescador Island, known for vibrant marine life and coral reefs.

10. Cebu Tarsier Sanctuary
Visit the Tarsier Sanctuary in Bohol (near Cebu) to see one of the world's smallest primates, the Philippine tarsier, in a natural conservation setting.
