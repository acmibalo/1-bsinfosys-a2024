Welcome to Palawan

Palawan, often referred to as the "Last Frontier" of the Philippines, is an archipelago of unparalleled beauty. With its lush forests, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life, Palawan is a top destination for nature lovers, beach enthusiasts, and adventure seekers.

Popular Spots in Palawan:
Some of Palawan's most breathtaking destinations include:

1. Underground River
The Puerto Princesa Underground River, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is one of the longest navigable underground rivers in the world. A boat tour through its cave system offers a unique and tranquil experience.

2. El Nido
El Nido is known for its dramatic limestone cliffs, crystal-clear lagoons, and pristine beaches. It's the perfect place for island hopping, snorkeling, and exploring secluded coves.

3. Coron
Famous for its crystal-clear lakes, lagoons, and incredible wreck diving spots, Coron is a diverâ€™s paradise. It's also home to scenic viewpoints and hidden lagoons.

4. Honda Bay
Honda Bay is perfect for island hopping and snorkeling, offering beautiful beaches and crystal-clear waters, along with islands like Starfish Island and Pandan Island.

5. Tubbataha Reefs Natural Park
A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Tubbataha is a marine sanctuary with some of the world's best diving spots, home to diverse marine life including sea turtles, sharks, and colorful coral gardens.
