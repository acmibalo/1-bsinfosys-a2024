Top Anime Genres

Action anime is full of intense battles, superheroes, and adrenaline-pumping moments. Popular examples include "Naruto," "Attack on Titan," and "My Hero Academia."

Romance anime tells heartfelt stories of love, relationships, and personal growth. Notable titles include "Your Name," "Toradora!" and "Clannad."

Slice of life anime depicts everyday life, focusing on characters and their personal development. Examples include "March Comes in Like a Lion" and "Barakamon."
