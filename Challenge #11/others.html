Red-Haired Pirates

Led by ”Red-Haired” Shanks, one of the Four Emperors, this crew is known for their incredible strength, camaraderie, and respect for freedom. They are a balanced and highly disciplined group, wielding immense influence across the seas without causing unnecessary destruction.

Bonney Pirates

Led by Jewelry Bonney, a Supernova with the ability to manipulate age using her Devil Fruit. The crew is relatively mysterious, known for their daring actions and Bonney’s personal vendetta against powerful figures like the World Government.

Big Mom Pirates

Ruled by Charlotte Linlin (Big Mom), another Emperor of the Sea, this crew is family-based, with many of Big Mom’s children holding key positions. They seek power through marriage alliances and control over territories, using Linlin’s Soul-Soul Fruit powers to instill fear.

Blackbeard Pirates

Led by Marshall D. Teach (Blackbeard), this crew is notorious for their treachery and ambition. Teach is the only known person to wield two Devil Fruits, and the crew is composed of highly dangerous and ruthless individuals seeking ultimate power.

Fallen Monk Pirates

Led by Urouge, a monk-like Supernova who uses his Devil Fruit powers to convert damage into strength. They are relatively enigmatic, known for their leader’s resilience and his ability to defeat one of Big Mom’s Sweet Commanders.

Heart Pirates

Led by Trafalgar D. Water Law, a Supernova and former Warlord of the Sea. The crew operates like a submarine unit, specializing in stealth and medical expertise. Law wields the powerful Ope Ope no Mi, which grants him incredible surgical and combat abilities.

Whitebeard Pirates

Led by the late Edward Newgate (Whitebeard), they were one of the most powerful pirate crews in history, known for their familial bond. Whitebeard’s Gura Gura no Mi gave him the power to create earthquakes, making him a near-unstoppable force.

Kid Pirates

Led by Eustass Kid, another Supernova, this crew is infamous for their violent reputation. Kid wields the power of the Jiki Jiki no Mi, which allows him to manipulate magnetism, and his ambition rivals Luffy’s in seeking the One Piece.

Fire Tank Pirates

Led by Capone “Gang” Bege, a Supernova with the Castle-Castle Fruit, which allows him to turn his body into a fortress. The crew operates like a mafia family, using cunning strategies and overwhelming firepower in battle.

Beast Pirates

Ruled by Kaido, one of the Four Emperors, this crew is known for their Zoan Devil Fruit users, many of whom have artificial Zoan abilities from SMILE fruits. They aim to create a world of war and chaos under Kaido’s rule.

Cross Guild

A new organization led by Buggy the Clown, Crocodile, and Dracule Mihawk, they place bounties on Marines, flipping the power dynamic of the world. Known for its unorthodox yet dangerous structure, the guild threatens the stability of the World Government.

Hawkins Pirates

The Hawkins Pirates are an infamous and notable rookie pirate crew introduced in Sabaody Archipelago around the time the Straw Hat Pirates arrived there two years ago. As such, they are part of the Worst Generation, with their captain being Basil Hawkins.
