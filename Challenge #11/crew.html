Straw Hat Pirates

The Straw Hat Pirates is a pirate crew originating from East Blue. The crew was started by Monkey D. Luffy, who serves as the main protagonist of One Piece. The crew got their name from the signature straw hat that Luffy wears, which was given to him by his pirate mentor Red-Haired Shanks. Before setting sail for the Grandline, the Straw Hat Pirates became the most notorious pirate crew in the East Blue by beating the likes of Don Kreig and the Arlong Pirates
