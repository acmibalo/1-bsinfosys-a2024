Libero

The role of the libero in volleyball is to:

1. Focus on receiving serves and playing defense.

2. Be the main receiver of serves and the second line of defense against attacks

3. Keep the ball in play and pass it to the setter for an attack.

4. Deliver precise passes to set up teammates for successful attacks.

Have excellent court vision, quick reflexes, and the ability to read the game
