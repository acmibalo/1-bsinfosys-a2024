Grandma and Grandpa Turns Young Again


Summary

Grandma and Grandpa, once vibrant and full of life, had grown old over the years, and now spent their days quietly in their cozy home. They loved telling their grandchildren stories of their youth and adventures. But deep down, they both longed for the days when they were young, full of energy and joy.

After eating a mysterious apple from their farm, an elderly pair of lovebirds, Shouzou and Ine, experience a miraculous transformation into their younger selves. Despite their newfound youth and beauty, the two continue in their cute senior ways. Witness an enduring bond that's stronger and more precious than ever before.
