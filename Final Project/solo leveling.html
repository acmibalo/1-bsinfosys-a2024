Solo Leveling


Summary

Jinwoo Sung, a weak and underwhelming hunter in a world where hunters, people with special abilities, fight monsters and explore dungeons. Jinwoo is known as the "World's Weakest Hunter" due to his lack of strength and ability. However, after a near-death experience in a dangerous dungeon, he is granted a mysterious power by an unknown system.

This power allows him to level up, just like a character in a video game, with no limits. He can gain strength, skills, and abilities by completing quests, killing monsters, and growing stronger at an unprecedented rate. As he trains and becomes more powerful, Jinwoo gradually rises from the weakest to one of the most powerful hunters in the world.

Along the way, he uncovers the dark secrets behind the system, the dungeons, and the true nature of the hunters and the monsters they face. Jinwoo must navigate dangerous battles, forge alliances with other hunters, and confront the mysterious forces that control the world of dungeons. His ultimate goal is to uncover the truth behind the system and its origins, all while protecting his loved ones and rising to the top of the hunter world.
