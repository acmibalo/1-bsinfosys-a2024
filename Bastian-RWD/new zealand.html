NEW ZEALAND

New Zealand is an island nation located in the southwestern Pacific Ocean, consisting of two main islands—North Island and South Island—along with numerous smaller islands. Known for its breathtaking landscapes, New Zealand features stunning mountains, lush forests, picturesque beaches, and unique geological formations, such as geysers and hot springs, particularly in areas like Rotorua.

The country is renowned for its indigenous Māori culture, which plays a significant role in New Zealand's identity. Visitors can experience traditional Māori customs, art, and language, enriching their understanding of the nation’s heritage.

New Zealand is also famous for its outdoor activities and adventure sports, attracting thrill-seekers to enjoy hiking, bungee jumping, skiing, and surfing. The diverse ecosystems support a variety of wildlife, including unique species like the kiwi bird
