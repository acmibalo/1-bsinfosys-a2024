ITALY

Italy, located in Southern Europe, is renowned for its rich history, art, and culture. Shaped like a boot, it is bordered by the Mediterranean Sea and is home to diverse landscapes, from the rolling hills of Tuscany to the stunning Amalfi Coast and the rugged Alps in the north.

Italy is famous for its contributions to art and architecture, with cities like Florence, Venice, and Rome showcasing masterpieces from the Renaissance, Baroque, and other periods. Iconic landmarks include the Colosseum, the Leaning Tower of Pisa, and the Vatican City, home to St. Peter’s Basilica and the Sistine Chapel.

Italian cuisine is celebrated worldwide, characterized by its regional diversity. Staples include pasta, pizza, olive oil, and wine, with each region offering its own specialties. The country’s rich culinary traditions are often accompanied by a strong emphasis on family and community.
