Dota 2

Dota 2 is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed by Valve. It is one of the most popular games in the esports scene.

Features of Dota 2

  • Over 120 unique heroes to choose from.
  • Intense 5v5 team battles.
  • Regular updates with new content and balance changes.

How to Play

  1. Select your hero from the vast roster.
  2. Work with your team to destroy the enemy's Ancient.
  3. Strategize, adapt, and dominate your opponents!
