What is Manga?

Manga refers to Japanese comic books or graphic novels, typically serialized in magazines. Known for its unique art style, it covers a wide variety of genres and has a dedicated global fanbase.

What is Manhua?

Manhua is a term used to describe Chinese-language comics, often similar to manga but with distinctive art styles and storytelling techniques. Manhua has gained popularity worldwide, especially with digital platforms.

Popular Manga and Manhua

Some popular manga include "Naruto," "One Piece," and "Attack on Titan," while well-known manhua include "The King's Avatar," "Battle Through the Heavens," and "Tales of Demons and Gods."
