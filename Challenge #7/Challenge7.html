About Us

Tired of the same old hair accessories? The Hair Haven was founded by Princess Maricar Diwa Clua at Kabacan, Cotabato on the 20th day in November 2024. At The Hair Haven, we believe in adding a touch of personality and flair to every hairstyle. We're not just about function; we're about making a statement. That's why we curate a collection of unique, high-quality hair accessories that you won't find anywhere else. We scour the globe for the latest trends and collaborate with independent designers to bring you pieces that are both stylish and durable.​

From statement headbands that turn heads to delicate barrettes that add a touch of elegance, we have something to elevate your look for every occasion. Whether you're dressing up for a special event, adding a touch of whimsy to your everyday style, or simply looking for a way to express your unique personality, we have the perfect accessories to help you shine.​

We believe that every woman deserves to feel confident and empowered, and we're here to help you unleash your inner style icon. Explore our collection and discover the perfect accessories to express your unique personality.
