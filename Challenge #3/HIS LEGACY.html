Technoblade: The King Never Dies

Alexander (June 1, 1999 – June 2022), known online as Technoblade, was an American YouTuber who became famous for his Minecraft videos and involvement in the Dream SMP. Technoblade’s content was known for its humor, skillful gameplay, and his deep connection with his audience.

He created a legacy that inspired millions and will always be remembered for his positive influence, humor, and unwavering strength during difficult times.

Even in his last moments, Technoblade’s words remain unforgettable: "The King Never Dies." This website is a tribute to his incredible journey and the impact he had on his fans and the gaming community.
