Top 10 Must-Visit Spots in Boracay

1. White Beach
This iconic 4-kilometer stretch is famous for its powdery white sand and crystal-clear waters. It's divided into three stations, each offering a unique atmosphere—Station 1 for luxury resorts, Station 2 for dining and shopping, and Station 3 for budget-friendly accommodations. Water sports like parasailing and jet skiing are popular here.

2. Puka Beach
Located on the northern part of the island, Puka Beach is known for its serene environment and rustic charm. Unlike White Beach, it features coarser sand made up of crushed shells, making it perfect for those seeking tranquility away from the crowds.

3. Ariel’s Point
A hotspot for adventure seekers, Ariel’s Point offers cliff diving into clear waters from varying heights (up to 15 meters). Accessible by boat, it's a great place to enjoy swimming, snorkeling, or simply relaxing in a beautiful setting.

4. Mount Luho
As the highest point in Boracay, Mount Luho provides breathtaking panoramic views of the island. The hike is manageable with concrete paths leading to several viewing decks, making it a popular spot for photos and enjoying nature.

5. Bulabog Beach
Known as the windsurfing and kitesurfing capital of Boracay, Bulabog Beach is ideal for water sports enthusiasts. The beach's strong winds make it a prime location for both beginners and experienced surfers.

6. Island Hopping Tours
Exploring nearby islands such as Crystal Cove Island and Crocodile Island is a must-do activity. These tours often include snorkeling opportunities and visits to secluded beaches, providing a full day of adventure.

7. Sunset Yacht Cruise
Experience Boracay's stunning sunsets aboard a yacht. These cruises often include dining options and are perfect for romantic evenings or relaxing with friends while enjoying the picturesque views.

8. Scuba Diving and Snorkeling
The waters around Boracay are teeming with marine life, making it an excellent destination for scuba diving and snorkeling. Popular dive sites include Crocodile Island and various coral gardens around the area.

9. Boracay Pub Crawl
For those looking to experience the nightlife, joining a pub crawl is a fun way to meet new people while exploring some of Boracay's best bars and clubs.

10. Crystal Cove Island
This small island offers beautiful beaches and unique caves to explore. It's less crowded than other spots, making it perfect for a peaceful day trip filled with exploration and relaxation.
