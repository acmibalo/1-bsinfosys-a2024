About Batanes

Batanes is a stunning province located in the northernmost part of the Philippines, known for its picturesque rolling hills, traditional Ivatan houses, and unique cultural heritage. The province offers a serene and peaceful escape with breathtaking landscapes and a rich history, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers and history enthusiasts.
