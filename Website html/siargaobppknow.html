Welcome to Siargao

Siargao is an island located in the northeastern part of Mindanao. Known as the "surfing capital of the Philippines," it is famous for its crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, and exciting surfing spots. Whether you're a thrill-seeker or a beach lover, Siargao offers something for everyone.

Popular Spots:
Some of Siargao's famous destinations include:

1. Cloud 9
Cloud 9 is the most famous surfing spot in Siargao, attracting surfers from all over the world. Known for its powerful waves and beautiful sunrise views, it's a must-visit for surfing enthusiasts.

2. Sugba Lagoon
Sugba Lagoon is a peaceful, turquoise water paradise perfect for swimming, paddleboarding, or simply relaxing by the water's edge. Surrounded by lush jungle, it's ideal for those seeking tranquility.

3. Magpupungko Rock Pools
These natural tidal pools are perfect for a relaxing swim. When the tide is low, the pools become crystal clear and are excellent for exploring marine life.

4. Siargao Island Hopping
Take an island-hopping tour to nearby islands like Guyam Island, Daku Island, and Naked Island, each offering pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and unforgettable views.

5. Tayangban Cave Pool
An adventure spot for thrill-seekers, Tayangban Cave offers an underground river tour with a refreshing swim in cool, clear water.
