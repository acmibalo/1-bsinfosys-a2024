EXPLORING CORON ISLAND

Coron Island: A Natural Paradise in Palawan, Philippines

Coron Island, located in northern Palawan, is one of the most stunning destinations in the Philippines, renowned for its pristine natural beauty. It is part of the Calamian Islands and is governed by the municipality of Coron. The island is home to dramatic limestone cliffs, turquoise lagoons, white-sand beaches, and rich marine biodiversity, making it a must-visit spot for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Key Features:
Lakes and Lagoons:

The island boasts several breathtaking lakes, with Kayangan Lake being the most famous. Often called the "cleanest lake in Asia," its crystal-clear waters surrounded by towering karst cliffs offer a serene environment for swimming and kayaking. Another notable spot is Barracuda Lake, popular among divers for its unique thermocline layers.
