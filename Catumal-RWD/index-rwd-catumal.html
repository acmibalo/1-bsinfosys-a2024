Cities

Cities are dynamic, diverse centers with distinct skylines, rich cultures, and robust infrastructures. They host a blend of architecture, thriving economic hubs, and vibrant social scenes.

While many offer green spaces and sustainable planning, cities also face challenges like overcrowding, pollution, and housing shortages. Each city has a unique character shaped by its history, residents, and role in the global landscape.
